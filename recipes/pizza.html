Pizza

Description

Pizza is a traditional Italian dish, usually containing wheat-based dough, tomato sauce and cheese.

There are many types of pizza. E.g. capricciosa, margherita and pepperoni.

It's an excellent choice for movie nights!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • (Tomato) sauce
  • Cheese
  • Ham
  • Champignons

Steps

  1. Make the dough by combining flour, water and salt.
  2. Spread the tomato sauce (or similar)
  3. Put cheese, ham and everything else you want on pizza.
