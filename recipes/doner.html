Doner

Description

Doner is a traditional Turkish dish. It is also very popular in Germany, as well as many other countries.

It is basically a sandwich consisting of chicken or beef meat, salad and some dressings.

The meat is cooked on a vertical rotisserie and sliced with a larg knife.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Chicken or Beef meat
  • Salad: onions, tomatoes, cucumbers, etc.
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Prepare the salad.
  3. Make the sandwich and top it with a desired sauce.
