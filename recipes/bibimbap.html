Bibimbap

Description

Bibimbap is a traditional Korean dish

It contains rice, many vegetables, fried egg and beef meat.

It is usually topped with soy sauce or similar.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Carrots
  • Kimchi
  • Beef
  • 1 Egg
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Cook the rice.
  2. Fry all the vegetables you got.
  3. Fry an egg.
  4. Prepare the meat.
  5. Put everything in a bowl and top it with soy sauce. Enjoy!
